About Us

Starting off with the opening of a Korean Fine-dining SURA in 2015, KorabiaFoodvill successfully launched the Korean BBQ & Grill "Hwaro," and expanded to the catering sector by promoting Korean food at house parties, corporate events, and K-pop performances to pave the way as the number one Korean food enterprise in Saudi Arabia.
